FAQ
Does my data save between sessions? ›
Not yet — this is a demo with no database. Everything resets on refresh. The real version will save to your account automatically.
How do I add a progress photo? ›
Progress photos are a Premium feature. Open a project, go to the Photos tab, and tap the upgrade prompt. Once subscribed, you can upload photos directly from your camera roll.
Can I use noodlr on desktop? ›
The demo works in any browser. A native iOS and Android app is planned — noodlr is designed mobile-first so it'll feel right at home.
What's the 5-project limit about? ›
Free accounts can track up to 5 projects across all statuses. Premium removes the limit entirely. Projects in Done status still count toward the cap.
How do I cancel Premium? ›
Go to Plans & Billing → Manage billing & invoices. You can cancel any time — your Premium access continues until the end of your billing period.
Is my data private? ›
Yes. Your projects are stored in your account only — we don't sell or share your data. A full privacy policy will be published before the public launch.
